The Bridge

End-to-End Platform - Workflows for every field action

A hybrid digital extension system combining field operations optimization with direct offline-enabled AI-driven communication with the farmers.

The solution is a scalable unified management platform designed to increase crop yields and streamline agricultural operations.

Yield Optimization:

The system boosts production by:

  • Digital training tools - 3D animated videos - to the extension officers.
  • Collecting real-time agronomic data from the field directly to a digital platform.
  • Regularly ongoing communication with the farmers through a human-like conversation, collecting data, and allowing access to immediate troubleshooting.

Scalable Management: The platform reduces the need for micromanagement. Instead of managers maintaining direct contact with every farmer, the system centralizes control.

Technological Integration: An automated communication with the farmers through AI-driven chatbots via SMS ensures meaningful interactions.

This ensures uninterrupted support and proactive problem-solving between physical field visits.

In the Field

How It Works in the Field

1

Digitize Workflows

We equip extension teams with digital workflows for farmer registration, training, follow-up, supervision, and data collection, improving accountability, visibility, and the structure and consistency of service delivery.

2

Facial Recognition

To overcome weak ID infrastructure, we utilize facial recognition to eliminate duplicate profiles and ensure accurate, targeted service delivery.

3

Visual Training

Additionally, we translate complex agronomic concepts into actionable guidance using engaging 3D visual training modules, specifically designed for low-literacy users.

4

AI Chatbots

Fully adaptable to local languages, our human-like AI chatbots maintain scalable, ongoing contact with farmers, identifying challenges early and delivering timely, relevant advice to drive growth.
