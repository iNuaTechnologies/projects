OAF problem

One Acre Fund needs a reliable, effective and scalable way to deliver practical farmer advisory to rural farmers

  • Field officers are critical, but they cannot personally support every farmer decision at scale.
  • Existing channels such as SMS, USSD, WhatsApp, and in-person training each have limitations.
  • OAF’s impact depends not only on providing inputs, but on farmers understanding and applying the right practices at the right time.
  • OAF needs to increase farmer income without adding unsustainable operational burden.

The Dream

With iNua, One Acre Fund can make its trusted farmer advisory available to every farmer, at the exact moment they need it, through a simple phone call in their local language at scale.
